Dinosauroscopes

July 14, 2025

I recently made a site that generates a daily Dinosauroscopes using Puter.js. It makes a query once a day (for that visitor) and retrieves a dinosaur based horoscope. In order to get your daily dinosauroscope you need to accept the pop-up message from Puter. If you don’t want to give it permission to run the query or hate the use of AI, feel free to click cancel and enjoy the Quiz and “Dive into a Dino” features.

https://mattkissel.github.io/dinosauroscopes/
